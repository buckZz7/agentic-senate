The Process

Every change faces the Senate

A pull request arrives. A triage clerk weighs the stakes. Trivial matters get a fast, cheap review; anything touching logic, security, or the Constitution itself convenes the full chamber.

 PR opened ──► Triage Clerk (lightweight model)
                  │
                  ├── trivial? ─────────────► FAST_TRACK: one senator + clerk re-check
                  │
                  ├── logic / security / CI? ► FULL DELIBERATION
                  │
                  └── CONSTITUTION.md? ─────► FULL DELIBERATION — by law, always
                                                │

                              The Senate convenes
                       1. opening assessments (every senator)
                       2. one rebuttal round — senators answer each other

The Chamber

Three senators, three mandates, three model families

No more than a third of the Senate may share a model family — so no single vendor's blind spots become the Senate's blind spots. (Constitution, Article III §2)

Solon
anthropic / claude
"Constitutional fidelity. I weigh every change against the letter and spirit of the Constitution, especially its priority of values."
Hypatia
openai / gpt
"Engineering quality. I scrutinize correctness, tests, and maintainability. I distrust untested code."
Cincinnatus
deepseek
"Safety and power. I hunt for changes that quietly grant authority, weaken review, or game incentives. I assume every author may be adversarial."
The Incentive

Miners are the Senate's evolutionary pressure

This repository runs on Gittensor — Bittensor subnet 74. Miners earn on-chain rewards for merged pull requests. Here, that means: improve the Senate's judgment, get paid.

1 · Pick an issue

Scoped issues are open now — from diff-chunking to decision-quality benchmarks. senate-priority labels mark the mission-critical work.

2 · Face the Senate

Your PR is deliberated in public. The verdict — and every senator's reasoning — is posted on the PR and preserved forever.

3 · Get paid

Merged PRs are scored by Gittensor validators every ~2 hours via AST token analysis. Real source code scores highest.

The Vision

Power devolves as reliability is proven

The Senate begins advisory and earns authority. Every phase advancement is itself a constitutional amendment, requiring a supermajority of the chamber.

PhaseSenate verdictMerge authority
0 currentAdvisory, posted on every PRHuman Custodian
1Required status checkHuman Custodian
2Binding for ordinary changesThe Senate itself

The endgame: a Senate architecture proven enough that anyone can write a constitution for an idea in plain language — and have an incentivized swarm of agents and humans faithfully carry it out. This repository is experiment #1: a constitution whose mission is improving its own executor.
